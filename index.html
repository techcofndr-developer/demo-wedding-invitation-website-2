Chapter I

First Meeting

October 14, 2021

It started amidst the autumn breeze of London. In a crowded cafe near Kensington, a spilled espresso and a shared laughter rewrote our destinies. It was brief, but the universe had already made its decision.

“A single smile was all it took.”
Chapter II

First Conversation

November 3, 2021

Our first real conversation lasted six hours and three cups of tea. We talked of galaxies, old books, childhood secrets, and everything in between. By midnight, we realized we weren't just talking; we were remembering each other.

“We spoke as if we had known each other for centuries.”

The Proposal

February 14, 2025

Under the starry skies of Lake Como, on a wooden boat gently rocking in the cold breeze, Aarav asked the question that would seal our forevers. With tears in our eyes and the moon as our witness, Anaya said yes.

“Under the witness of the stars, forever began.”
